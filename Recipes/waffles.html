Waffles

Home made simple waffles

These home made waffles as quick and easy to make with simple ingredients. Cook to a golden brown and serve with butter or you your favorite syrup.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup milk (2%)
  • 6 tbs sugar
  • 6 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs vanila
  • 2 tbs vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Preheat waffle press
  2. Mix flour, baking powder and sugar in medium mixing bowl.
  3. Mix water, milk, eggs, vanila and oil in separate large mixing bowl and wisk together
  4. Slowly add dry ingredients into wet ingredients while wisking together.
  5. Pour half cup of batter onto heated waffle press and close press.
  6. Remove when golden brown and serve hot with butter and/or syrup.
