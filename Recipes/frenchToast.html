French Toast

Simple, no fail, French toast

Easy to make frech toast with simple ingredient list. This is a easy to follow french toast recipe and one that is sure to please.

Ingredients

  • 10 thick slices of white bread
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter milk
  • 1 tbs vannila
  • 4 tbs sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Preheat grill or skillet to 300 degrees F
  2. Wisk together all ingredients
  3. Dip bread into mix ensuring both sides are soaked in french toast mix.
  4. Place bread onto heated skillet until golden brown then fip and let other side cook to golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with powder sugar, syrup and/or fruit.
